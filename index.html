The Challenge

Why We Act Now

India is the world's diabetes capital. In the Coimbatore region, many only discover they are diabetic after serious complications have already set in.

Late Detection

Many residents only discover they are diabetic after complications arise, leading to preventable suffering and higher treatment costs.

Lack of Awareness

Regular preventative testing like A1C is not a common practice due to costs and lack of awareness about its importance.

Cultural Barriers

Health information is often unavailable in accessible Tamil-language formats that resonate with local dietary and lifestyle habits.

Our Approach

The Nalam Model

A three-pillar approach to shift the health paradigm from late-stage treatment to early-stage prevention.

Free A1C Testing

Partnering with vetted local laboratories to provide gold-standard blood sugar testing at no cost to the community.

Learn more

Targeted Education

Bilingual Tamil/English guidance on nutrition, local foods to eat and avoid, and exercise routines tailored to the community.

Learn more

Data-Driven Follow-up

WhatsApp-based tracking to monitor high-risk individuals and send timely reminders for 3 or 6-month re-testing.

Learn more
